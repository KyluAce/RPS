\subsection{Zadanie 1}

Dystrybuanta zmiennej losowej $F_X(t)$:

$$
F_X(t) = \left\{
\begin{array}{ll}
0 & \text{gdy} \quad t < 0\\
\frac{2}{3} & \text{gdy} \quad 0 \leq t < 1\\
1 & \text{gdy} \quad 1 \leq t
\end{array}
\right.
$$

$F_X(-1) = 0$, $F_X(0) = \frac{2}{3}$, $F_X(0.5) = \frac{2}{3}$,
$F_X(1) = 1$, $F_X(2) = 1$
